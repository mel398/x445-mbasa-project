Our Story

Keren and Khoa are high school sweethearts who grew up together in the heart of the Dr. Phillips community. From a young age, they dreamed of building a big, joyful family and one day opening an ice cream shop that felt just as warm and welcoming. In 2015, their journey into parenthood began with the birth of their first son, Jackson, and shortly after came the launch of their family YouTube channel , KKandbabyJ. Over the years, their family continued to grow with Landon, Sutton, Hanson, and finally their youngest and only daughter, Magnolia. Her sweet nickname, “Nola,” became the perfect inspiration for the name of their long-envisioned ice cream shop—a tribute to love, family, and the joy of sharing something special. Nola’s Ice Cream is a celebration of that vision, brought to life in collaboration with renowned ice cream shop owner Marie Mercado, the creative force behind The Greenery Creamery and Sampaguita. Together, they’ve crafted a place that’s as thoughtful and inviting as it is delicious. Rooted in community, family, and a shared love for great dessert, Nola’s aims to serve the Dr. Phillips neighborhood with hand-crafted ice cream that’s bursting with flavor, quality, and a little bit of magic. Whether you’re a local or just passing through, we hope every scoop makes you feel right at home. Thank you for being apart out ours. Nabe is Keren and Khoa’s cozy, modern take on Japanese hot pot where fresh ingredients, flavorful broths, and good company come together in every meal. It’s the kind of place where you can slow down, cook at the table, and share laughs over steaming bowls and sizzling bites. Nabe is all about family, community, and creating memorable moments—one hot pot at a time.

Keren and Khoa are high school sweethearts who grew up just down the street from each other in the Dr. Phillips community of Orlando. They met as teenagers and quickly became inseparable, bonding over silly jokes, late-night talks, and dreams of building a big, happy life together. Whether they were studying for exams or sneaking out for ice cream dates, their story has always been full of laughter and love. As the years went on, their love story grew into something even sweeter. From starting a family to sharing their lives on YouTube, Keren and Khoa built a community that followed their journey every step of the way. Nola’s Ice Cream is a dream come true for them—a place to bring people together, share joy, and celebrate the simple things (like a really good scoop of ice cream).

Jackson. The oldest of the bunch, Jackson is kind-hearted, curious, and always ready to help. He’s thoughtful and mature for his age, often looking out for his younger brothers with a big-brother sense of responsibility and love.

Landon. Landon is full of charm and spunk. He’s playful, outspoken, and loves to keep everyone laughing with his big personality and sense of humor.

Sutton. Sutton is the cuddly one—sweet, silly, and always smiling. He adores his older brothers and brings a gentle joy to the house with his easygoing nature.

Hansen. Hansen is curious and observant, always watching the world with wide eyes and a quiet confidence. He’s growing into his own personality and adds a peaceful presence to the crew.

Magnolia. Meet our spunky CEO, Nola. She is joyful, bubbly, and full of light. She’s doted on by her brothers and brings everyone together with her adorable giggles and endless sweetness.
