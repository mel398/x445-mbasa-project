Our Story

Keren and Khoa are high school sweethearts who grew up together in the heart of the Dr. Phillips community. From a young age, they dreamed of building a big, joyful family and one day opening an ice cream shop that felt just as warm and welcoming. In 2015, their journey into parenthood began with the birth of their first son, Jackson, and shortly after came the launch of their family YouTube channel, KKandbabyJ. Over the years, their family continued to grow with Landon, Sutton, Hanson, and finally their youngest and only daughter, Magnolia. Her sweet nickname, “Nola,” became the perfect inspiration for the name of their long-envisioned ice cream shop—a tribute to love, family, and the joy of sharing something special.

Nola’s Ice Cream is a celebration of that vision, brought to life in collaboration with renowned ice cream shop owner Marie Mercado, the creative force behind The Greenery Creamery and Sampaguita. Together, they’ve crafted a place that’s as thoughtful and inviting as it is delicious. Rooted in community, family, and a shared love for great dessert, Nola’s aims to serve the Dr. Phillips neighborhood with hand-crafted ice cream that’s bursting with flavor, quality, and a little bit of magic. Whether you’re a local or just passing through, we hope every scoop makes you feel right at home. Thank you for being apart out ours.
